Midnight Wanderer

Through city streets when darkness falls,
I walk alone past neon calls,
The streetlights paint a golden glow
On empty paths where shadows flow.

Windows gleam with stories untold,
Of lives lived out, both young and old,
While somewhere in the distance far,
A lonely dog begins to bark.

The night wind whispers secrets deep,
To those who cannot find their sleep,
And in this quiet, peaceful hour,
I find within myself, my power.

So here I roam through midnight's embrace,
Finding beauty in this quiet space,
Where solitude becomes my friend,
Until the dawn brings night's sweet end.
