## 🎨 Visual Effects Breakdown

### **Glassmorphism** 
Frosted glass panels with depth
- `backdrop-filter: blur(20px) saturate(180%)`
- Subtle borders and inset highlights
- Modern macOS-style depth

### **Living Gradients**
Colors that shift and breathe
- `animation: gradientShift 8s ease infinite`
- Multi-color gradients that move
- Applied to text and backgrounds

### **Magnetic Interactions**
Elements that respond to hover
- `transform: translateY(-4px) scale(1.01)`
- Smooth spring physics
- Lift + scale + shadow combo

### **Pulsing Glows**
Subtle glows for active states
- `animation: glowPulse 2s ease-in-out infinite`
- Color-matched shadows
- Draws attention without being annoying

### **Premium Inputs**
Inputs that feel responsive
- Lift on focus (`translateY(-2px)`)
- Glowing focus rings
- Smooth transitions
